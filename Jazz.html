Jazz

Jazz, a well-known music genre, was first introduced by a group that called themselves the Original Dixieland Jass Band in 1917. Artists such as Sidney Bechet, Fletcher Henderson, and Billie Holiday introduced elements such as various instruments to Jazz and helped in popularizing Jazz in the 20th century.

The most common instruments used for Jazz are: the trumpet, saxophone, trombone, piano, bass, and guitar.
