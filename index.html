What are Music Generes?

A music genre refers to the categorization system that defines pieces of music and their distinc styles and elements. The songs that are included in a genere "share similarities in their forms, styles, and/or origins". Elements in specific genres include "the instruments used, the country of origin, the style of form" and even the time frame and reason for the the music being created. There are hundreds of music genres that also include sub-genres in order to have a more accurate classification. Featured below are the the top 25 most popular music genres.
