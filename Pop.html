The genre of Pop, also known as popular music, is a "style of music that appeared in the 60s in the United States and initially appreciated by teenages, which is why the songs were often and are used to the rhythms associated with the dance, and they are mostly short songs about love". Pop music is also known as light music, meaning that it is "relatively simple music, cathcy and easy to comprehend. whose main purpose is to entertain, to sing and make people dance. Though, pop music have developed over time from the time it appeared, going through what is reffered to as three revolutions in a span of 50 years.
